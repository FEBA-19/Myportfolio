Smart Waste Segregation Unit

Smart Waste Segregation Unit

Waste segregation is a critical aspect of sustainable waste management, contributing significantly to recycling and reducing environmental pollution. Traditional waste segregation methods often rely on manual sorting, which is labor-intensive, time-consuming, and prone to errors. This project presents a Smart Waste Segregation Unit that utilizes deep learning techniques to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of waste sorting. The proposed system employs convolutional neural networks (CNNs) trained on extensive datasets comprising various waste categories. Through image recognition and classification, the CNN model identifies different types of waste items particularly metal,organic,recyclable and disposable waste with high precision. The system integrates this deep learning model into a smart sorting mechanism, capable of sorting waste items into appropriate categories automatically. A prototype of the automated waste segregation system is developed and tested.Also the prototype is incorporated with bin level detection and smell detection sensors where the bin levels can be monitored by the user using blynk app. The integration of deep learning technology into waste management systems offers a scalable and sustainable solution for efficient waste segregation, thereby contributing to a cleaner environment and promoting a circular economy.

Sanitary Napkin Incinarator

Sanitary Napkin Incinarator

Improper disposal of the sanitary pad can lead to various problems which include large heaps of pads all over, bacteria causing infection, unhygienic environment etc.The used sanitary pad that have not been disposed of properly sometimes block the drainage system. Incineration is a better technique to dispose of menstrual waste.To incinerate sanitary napkins, electrical or physical fire based incinerators can be used. Toilet facilities in India are very poor as they lack bins for the disposal of sanitary pads.As we are focusing more on rural areas our proposed system focuses on disposing menstrual waste by burning napkin using solar power based burner, which burns the napkins into ashes within minutes.
